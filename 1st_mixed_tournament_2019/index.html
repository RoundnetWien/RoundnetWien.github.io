First Vienna Mixed Tournament

Registration is closed

More than 25 teams signed up - Thank you and we are excited to welcome you at the 1st Vienna Mixed Roundnet Tournament!

Most important facts:

Location: Grenzackerstraße 23, 1100 Wien (Public transport station: bus 15A Daumegasse or underground U1 Altes Landgut)
9am - 9:30am Check-In and Warm-Up
9:30am - 9:45am Opening announcements
9:45am - 1pm Group play
1pm - 2pm Lunch Break
2pm - 5:30pm Bracket Play

The ‘1st Vienna Mixed Roundnet Tournament’ will follow the EUSRA rules (https://www.eusra.com/official-rules). We will have a printed version at the tournament as well. Photos taken at the tournament may be published.

For lunch break, you could either bring some food with you or you can buy your lunch at one of the two supermarkets that are both really close to the tournament venue:

HOFER: Daumegasse 13, 1100 Vienna

SPAR (rather small): Grenzackerstraße 2-4, 1100 Vienna

As the water quality in Vienna is exceptionally good, we invite you to bring a refillable bottle with you, as you can drink the tab water at the tournament venue.

After the tournament, there will be dinner and afterparty at ‘Das Columbus’ which is only three underground stations away from the tournament venue.

Location: Columbuspl. 6, 1100 Wien

Public transport station: U1 Keplerplatz We hope to see you there!

If you have any questions, do not hesitate to contact us (wiener.roundnet@gmail.com)!

See you on Saturday, 1. Wiener Roundnet Verein #feelthespike
