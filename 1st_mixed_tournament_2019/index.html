First Indoor Tournament

We are really happy to invite you to the 1st Vienna Mixed Roundnet Tournament! On Saturday, the 5th of October 2019, we will enjoy a day full of Roundnet. It is a mixed tournament and we will begin with pool play in the morning and continue with bracket play in the afternoon. The tournament will be on grass. After the tournament, we would be happy to finish this great day together with you while having a dinner and celebrating near the tournament location. Join us! - We are looking forward to welcome you in Vienna at our tournament!
